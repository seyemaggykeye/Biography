About

Transforming security challenges into strategic advantages

I am a cybersecurity professional with over six years of experience delivering robust, risk-aligned security solutions across a wide range of organisational environments, from agile small and medium-sized enterprises to Tier 1 global financial institutions.

My career has provided a broad and practical perspective on cybersecurity, shaped by hands-on experience across multiple operating models. I began my professional journey within an SME, where I was responsible for implementing security controls in resource-constrained environments. This experience developed a strong appreciation for pragmatic, cost-effective security approaches that balance risk reduction with business enablement.

I subsequently transitioned into cybersecurity consulting, supporting multiple clients across diverse industries. In this role, I refined my ability to rapidly assess security postures, identify material risks, and design tailored security solutions aligned to each organisation's risk appetite, regulatory obligations, and strategic objectives.

I currently work as a Cybersecurity Architect at a Tier 1 global financial institution in London, where I design and implement enterprise-scale security architectures protecting critical financial systems and infrastructure. This role has further strengthened my expertise in security architecture design, regulatory compliance, threat modelling, and defence-in-depth strategies.

My contributions include leading and supporting initiatives across cloud security architecture design and reviews, zero-trust advisory, and comprehensive security architecture assessments. I have extensive experience in threat modelling and risk assessment for complex enterprise systems, as well as applying generative AI capabilities to enhance defensive security use cases, within one of the world's most demanding and highly regulated security environments.
